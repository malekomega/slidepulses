Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile information provided through Google or Microsoft sign-in. We also collect usage data such as presentations created and session activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve SlidePlus services, authenticate your identity, enable presentation features, and communicate important updates about the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure. Presentation data, audience responses, and account information are encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google and Microsoft authentication services for sign-in. These providers have their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Audience Data

When audience members participate in live sessions, we collect their responses (poll votes, quiz answers, word cloud submissions). Anonymous participation does not require personal information.

6. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your account and data at any time through the Settings page. You may also contact us to request complete data deletion.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at info@slideplus.io.
